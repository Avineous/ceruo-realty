Frequently Asked Questions

What areas of Dubai do you cover?

We specialise in Dubai Marina, Downtown Dubai, Business Bay, Palm Jumeirah, Jumeirah Lake Towers (JLT) and DIFC.

Do you handle sales and rentals?

Yes — CeruO covers buying, selling and leasing for residential and commercial properties.

Are these listings real?

No — this is a demo site for portfolio and AI agent testing. Listings are fictional and for demonstration purposes only.

How do I book a viewing?

Use the "Book a Viewing" button on a property page or contact us at hello@ceruorealty.demo.

What documents are available for each property?

Each property page contains a downloadable brochure (PDF) and, where available, virtual tour thumbnails and media.

Do you provide property valuations?

We provide data-backed pricing guidance and sample automated valuation ranges (AVM) in our demo content.

How do I contact CeruO Realty?

For enquiries, use the contact form on the website or email hello@ceruorealty.demo. For faster replies, use the WhatsApp links on listing pages.

Can I request a customised market report?

In a live deployment CeruO would provide customised market reports — for this demo, we include sample market data and snapshots.

Is CeruO licensed to operate in Dubai?

This is a demo site only. For real operations, always ensure the agent or agency is RERA licensed and authorised in the UAE.

Can I download brochures?

Yes — property pages link to sample brochure PDFs in the /downloads folder for demo purposes.

How do I report an issue or broken listing?

Email our demo support at hello@ceruorealty.demo and include the listing name and URL.

Do you provide virtual tours?

Our demo pages include virtual tour thumbnails. In live setups, integrations often use Matterport, 3D tours or embedded video.

Which currencies and payment terms are typically used?

Dubai property prices are commonly shown in AED (United Arab Emirates Dirham). Payment terms vary by developer and listing type.

Can a non-resident buy property in Dubai?

Yes, foreigners can buy in designated freehold areas in Dubai. This demo does not provide legal advice — consult a qualified attorney for transactions.

How accurate is pricing in the demo?

The demo uses sample pricing for illustration only. Do not rely on these figures for actual valuation or purchase decisions.

Where can I find more information about Copilot or AI agent integration?

Technical details and instructions for connecting Copilot Studio assistants will be provided in the project documentation / knowledge base.

Does CeruO handle property management?

Yes — sample workflows in the demo cover tenant maintenance requests, vendor scheduling, and reporting for landlords.

Do you offer relocation assistance?

In a real agency, relocation services (visas, schooling, move coordination) can be offered; this demo highlights the concept only.
